By Category

Cross-chain/Interoperability: $42.5M+

The highest-value category. Bridges and messaging protocols carry systemic risk, and bounty programs reflect this.

Liquid Staking: $21M+

Lido dominates, but EigenLayer, Swell, and others add significant coverage. Restaking creates new attack surfaces.

L2s: $10M+

Optimism, Arbitrum, ZKsync, Scroll, Linea all have active programs. The L2 ecosystem is well-funded for security.

DEXs: $8M+

Uniswap, Curve, and newer entrants like CoW Protocol. AMMs remain critical infrastructure.

Where the Opportunities Are

For Bug Hunters

  • Cross-chain protocols offer the highest payouts due to systemic risk
  • L2 infrastructure is well-funded and actively seeking vulnerabilities
  • Newer protocols (ZKsync era, Scroll) may have undiscovered issues as they mature

For Protocols

  • Bug bounties are now standard for DeFi — not having one is a red flag
  • Average max bounty for major protocols: $2-5M
  • Cross-chain projects lead in bounty size, reflecting both risk and TVL
